Youtube Sentiment Analysis


the process of analyzing and understanding the sentiment or emotions expressed by users in the comments, likes, dislikes, and other interactions on YouTube videos.The main objective of YouTube sentiment analysis is to determine whether the sentiment expressed by users is positive, negative, or neutral towards a particular video or topic.



Categorize

Comments Fetching

The process of retrieving the comments left by users on a particular video or channel. This can be done using YouTube's API (Application Programming Interface), which allows developers to programmatically access and retrieve data from the YouTube platform.

Sentiment Analysis

Sentiment analysis in YouTube involves analyzing the sentiment or emotions expressed by users in comments, likes, dislikes, and other interactions on YouTube videos. The goal is to understand the overall sentiment of the audience towards a particular video or topic.

Spam Detection

Spam detection in YouTube involves identifying and filtering out irrelevant, misleading, or malicious content that violates YouTube's policies and guidelines. It aims to maintain the quality and integrity of the platform by preventing the dissemination of spammy or harmful content.
