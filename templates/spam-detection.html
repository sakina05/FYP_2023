Spam Detection

YouTube spam detection is an algorithm that automatically identifies and filters out spam comments on YouTube videos, ensuring a cleaner and more relevant comment section for users. It uses various techniques like content analysis, pattern recognition, and machine learning to accurately identify and flag spam comments. Spam detection in YouTube involves identifying and filtering out irrelevant, misleading, or malicious content that violates YouTube's policies and guidelines.
